    • Alison Cree: researcher focused on the physiology and ecology of reptiles, particularly tuatara and lizards, with an emphasis on conservation.
    • Raymond B. Huey: Known for his work on thermal biology and ecology in lizards, particularly how they regulate their body temperatures in varying environments.
    • Martin Whiting: His research focuses on animal behavior and evolution, with a particular interest in social systems of lizards like the Australian tree skink.
