"Why are lizards objectively the best animal?"

Lizard is the common name used for all squamate reptiles other than snakes.
Lizards live in almost all contintents (except Antartica) and are hardy explorers. With their scaly skin, four legs and tails, they can be quite agile adventurers!

All about lizards!

  1. Main Characteristics
    • Habitat: Lizards inhabit a variety of environments, from deserts and forests to urban areas.
    • Diet: Most lizards are carnivorous, feeding on insects, small animals, or eggs, though some species are herbivorous
    • Age range: The average age depends heavily on the breed of the lizard. Geckos can live for 10/15 years, chameleons are 5/7 years.
      Of course, this does not contain lizard wizards that have achieved immortality.
  2. Secondary Characteristics
    • Movement: Some are able to run on water as they are just so speedy!
    • Escape: Some lizards are able to detach their tails but not all will regenerate them later.

This means that having lizards in your home is objectively a great idea, as it eats all the annoying bugs in your bedroom, allowing you to sleep well, knowing they guard your dreams.

H3: Why lizards make the best wizards!

  • 1, Cold-Blooded Nature: Their ability to regulate body temperature based on their surroundings reflects a cool, calm demeanor, perfect for a wizard mastering the balance of elements.
  • 2, Regeneration: Been hit? Some lizards can regenerate their tails after losing them, symbolizing a wizard's ability to heal, restore, or reinvent themselves through magic.
