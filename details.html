Why are lizards objectively the best animal?

Lizard is the common name used for all squamate reptiles other than snakes.
Lizards live in almost all continents (except Antarctica) and are hardy explorers. With their scaly skin, four legs, and tails, they can be quite agile adventurers!

All about lizards!

  1. Main Characteristics
    • Habitat: Lizards inhabit a variety of environments, from deserts and forests to urban areas.
    • Diet: Most lizards are carnivorous, feeding on insects, small animals, or eggs, though some species are herbivorous.
    • Age range: The average age depends heavily on the breed of the lizard. Geckos can live for 10/15 years, chameleons are 5/7 years.
  2. Secondary Characteristics
    • Movement: Some are able to run on water as they are just so speedy!
    • Escape: Some lizards can detach their tails, but not all will regenerate them later.

This means that having lizards in your home is objectively a great idea, as they eat all the annoying bugs in your bedroom.

Why lizards make the best wizards!

  • Cold-Blooded Nature: Perfect for a wizard mastering the balance of elements.
  • Regeneration: Symbolizing a wizard's ability to heal and restore.
